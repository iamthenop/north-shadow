# Part 5: Boring Transitions
### PQC Readiness the Boring Way: Sustainable Migration Without Heroics

Cryptography does not change all at once.

Algorithms age. Systems adjust. Replacement happens during routine service.

Post-quantum cryptography will likely follow the same path.

There will be no single moment of migration. Only gradual wear and steady replacement.

Organizations that treat this as a crisis will struggle.
Organizations that treat it as maintenance will adapt.

The most successful transitions rarely produce a memorable migration event.

They simply result in a system that looks normal again.
